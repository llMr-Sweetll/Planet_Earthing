# Claim Ladder

This ladder prevents the project from overclaiming.

## Level 0: Instrument Works

Claim:

> The sensor stack records stable plant-adjacent signals.

Required:

- dummy channel,
- baseline recordings,
- event markers,
- artifact notes.

## Level 1: Plant Responds To Touch

Claim:

> This plant/species shows measurable responses to standardized touch under our setup.

Required:

- object or robot touch,
- no-touch baseline,
- repeat sessions,
- pressure logs.

## Level 2: Human Conditions Differ

Claim:

> Host and stranger sessions differ in plant-derived features.

Required:

- host and stranger sessions matched on pressure, script, time, and environment,
- mixed-effects model,
- grouped ML holdout,
- confound-only comparison.

## Level 3: Familiarity-Associated Difference

Claim:

> The difference is associated with familiarity, not only measured physical confounds.

Required:

- repeated non-host condition,
- novel stranger condition,
- voice/playback or human-nearby control,
- replication across plants.

## Level 4: Plant Recognition

Claim:

> The plant recognizes or discriminates a familiar human individual.

Required:

- strong Level 3 evidence,
- independent replication,
- alternative explanations explicitly tested,
- cautious language and peer review.

## Disallowed Without Extraordinary Evidence

Do not claim:

- plants read thoughts,
- plants detect emotional intent,
- plants feel affection or fear,
- plants have human-like consciousness,
- a classifier proves sentience.
